## Objective of 3-Ball Billiards

<li><strong>General Rule:</strong> All fouls result in one of each player’s balls being placed back on the table.</li> <li><strong>Rejoining the Game:</strong> If you’ve been eliminated and a remaining player fouls, one of your balls is put back on the table, and you rejoin the game.</li> <li><strong>Contact Rule:</strong> You must hit an opponent’s ball first.
